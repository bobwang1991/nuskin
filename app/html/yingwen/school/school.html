We provide and recruit non-sales positions only at headquarters and all branches of NU SKIN Greater China.

NU SKIN Greater China Global Campus Recruitment project began in 2012. Over these past four years, we have made tremendous progress crossing over 4 continents (Asia, Europe, North America and Oceania) to recruit top talents. Our mission and target are focus on the International Chinese students, especially those overseas returnees students who speak fluent Mandarin & English, and would like to go back to Greater China for work at NU SKIN after graduation. Our goal is to provide all interested students begin their internship experience in vacation with NU SKIN and then turn to be our potential permanent staff in the future due to rapid business growth in Greater China in the past years. We are also interested in providing some of employment opportunities/management trainee program to graduating students.

In the midst of all the expected young talents, NU SKIN is looking for those who present particular NU SKIN DNA. The top four characteristics we look for in a person are ambition, good-hearted, confidence, and enthusiasm. We evaluate the presence of these qualities through many aspects such as the students’ involvement in campus clubs, student union leadership roles, study abroad experience, scholarship award experience, volunteer/community service participation, and other extracurricular activities.

NU SKIN is especially unique in its career platform and investment in young talents. What we have done is maximize the management trainee pool to all campus graduates who join the company, not just a selected few, to start his or her career right away as an entry level management position. The distinctive aspect of NU SKIN’s campus recruitment is that it recruits globally for the brightest and most brilliant talents back to the corporate office in Shanghai to fill these positions. Not only does the company value graduates who have cross-cultural background, the Greater China branch opts to provide a global perspective to all of its staff through the possibility of relocation, direct-communication with the top management team, global events support, and project tasks with co-workers from multiple backgrounds. In addition to the dynamic environment, the career progression is aggressive and hopeful for ambitious individuals. In order to support this plan, the company has enlarged the management pool capacity to all campus graduates joining the company.

Through the past years of campus recruitment process, NU SKIN has shown its tremendous potential to be an even more competitive company in the Greater China region. Students from worldwide have also commented on how our global recruitment method is advanced to them compared to other companies. We are continuously making a positive impact and being cutting edge in all aspects from products, culture, people, and opportunity.
