Founded in Provo, Utah in 1984, NU SKIN is one of the global leading companies and anti-aging expert in personal care products and nutrition supplements. In 1996, NU SKIN listed its shares on the New York Stock Exchange, with its stock code ‘NUS’.

By specially integrating advanced technology facilities, and a powerful team of scientists, NU SKIN aims to build a world-class anti-aging scientific research center, and continues to develop superior and innovative anti-aging products for the market. In 2003, NU SKIN officially entered China. In 2012, China mainland, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Macao regional markets have been integrated into NU SKIN Greater China, which now includes 32 branches and 54 NU SKIN plazas, stores and boutiques and 5 production bases. In April 2014, the largest over-seas investment in NU SKIN`S history (over 450 million RMB), the NU SKIN Greater China Innovation Park (GCIP), was opened in Fengxian, Shanghai.

NU SKIN has been growing solidly for over 30 years, spreading its business to 54 markets across the world, NU SKIN is differentiated by its ability to demonstrate that they truly have the best people, product, culture, and opportunity in the anti-aging, skin care, and wellness industries，it can be as big and successful as you want it to be.
